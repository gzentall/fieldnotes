What this is

Fintech XD is taking on exploratory work to look for opportunities in the lending platform, meaning the parts of lending that can be shared across products rather than built separately for each one.

This document lays out where we intend to look and why.

Why now

Several lending efforts are moving at the same time, across different products. The Consumer Group is offering new lines of credit to customers next year. Revenue based financing is being built from the ground up. Embedded lending is still maturing. Each one has to solve the same basic problems, like how a customer gets funded and how they manage what they owe, and today each is solving them on its own.

The longer that continues, the more these experiences drift apart, and the harder it is to bring them back together later. Doing the platform work now lets us shape these efforts while they are still being built, instead of cleaning up after. That is why it matters now, and it is worth our time.

A few principles guide this work. We think they are worth adopting across the teams working in lending:

  • Shared pieces are built once, so any product can use them instead of building their own.
  • Lending feels like a natural part of each product, and works the same way underneath.
  • As customers move across Intuit products, their experience carries over, rather than starting over.
Sheet 02 · Areas we are exploring

Where we intend to look

These are the areas we are starting with. We will sharpen where we focus as the work progresses.

Origination

How people find, apply for, and receive credit across the different places lending shows up.

Servicing

How people manage a loan or line of credit after they have it, and stay in good standing.

Embedded experiences

How lending shows up inside other products without each team having to rebuild it.

Reach into consumer products

Whether and how lending could extend further into consumer products over time.

Sheet 03 · Scope and open items

What this covers, and where we go next

What this covers

In focus The shared parts of lending that more than one product could use.
Not in focus right now Work that is specific to a single product, and decisions about solutions, methods, or timing. Those come later.

We will sharpen these boundaries as the work develops.

Open items

  • Which areas to focus on first. We will gather input from the team.
  • How to make the work useful. We are exploring ways to make the output valuable to stakeholders.
  • Who joins us. We are looking for collaborators on the work.
